Welcome to ScholarTrack

ScholarTrack is a Student Management System that allows Students, Teachers, and Parents to log in with their own account ID and view report cards, scores, attendance, and overall academic performance in one secure platform.

Vision: To create a transparent bridge between school, students, and home through accessible academic data.

Mission: To simplify performance tracking and improve communication between teachers, students, and parents.
